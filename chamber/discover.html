This is an aerial view of Lima, Peru. Especially during the summertime, Lima is very beautiful with lots of green, azure skies, and cotton clouds.

The Parque Ecologico Loma Amarilla is a natural land elevation with approx. 7.5 hectares, composed of flagstone. The park is a nice place to relax, wander around or enjoy the beautiful gardens and of course the small waterfall

The Larco Museum is a privately own museum of pre-Columbian art. It showcases chronological galleries that provide a thorough overview of 5,000 years of Peruvian pre-Columbian history. It is well known for its gallery of pre-Columbian erotic pottery.

In May 2007 a water and light show called the Circuito Magico del Agua (The Magic Water Circuit) was inaugurated. The Magic Water Circuit has the Guinness Book record for the largest fountain complex in the world, displaying 13 distinct fountains and many are interactive.

Laguna rapagna is lake located in Lima. It is located at an elevation of 15928 ft above the sea level. This is one of the places outside urban Lima. It attracts turists interested in doing outdoor activities.

Apu Siqay, a destination that is located 3937 ft. above sea level, where you will see the sky like never before. It is said that this tourist attraction has become one of the most popular for photography lovers, since this place has an unbeatable view, which provides the best images.
